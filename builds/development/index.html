About Me

A highly creative Graphic/Web Designer with 13 years of professional expertise in search of a new adventure.

Driven by the creative motto “You can only make a first impression once!” I have always aimed to give my clients that special edge that makes them stand out in a crowded marketplace. My strengths include but are not limited to (1) A strong foundation in classic & contemporary graphic & web design; (2) Finding inventive solutions to a client’s marketing design strategy. Drawing from my nerdy interests in pop culture e.g. graphic novels, books, movies & music I am not afraid to present more playful designs should a client wish to explore such. (3) The ability to communicate effectively with management, clients & the public at all levels; (4) Being originally from South Africa, a strong sense of gender & cultural sensitivity; (5) An insatiable thirst to learn everything I possibly can & apply it where needed; (6) A great sense of humor.
